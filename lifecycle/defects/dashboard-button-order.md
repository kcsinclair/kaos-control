# Dashboard Action Buttons in Wrong Order

## Reproduction Steps

1. Open the application and navigate to the dashboard.
2. Observe the two action buttons displayed on the dashboard.

## Expected Behaviour

The buttons should be ordered left to right as: **New Idea** (left), **New Defect** (right).

## Actual Behaviour

The buttons are displayed in the reverse order: **New Defect** appears on the left and **New Idea** appears on the right.
