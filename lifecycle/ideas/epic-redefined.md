The concept of Epics is a little different with agentic development, you can get so many things done in a release, it makes less sense.

However, what is great for humans to put multiple ideas into a single request, and then the agents have more to work on, and you get through more things as you go.

Idea: single concept, or related concepts per artefact.
Epic: many concepts in a single artefact, probably related but may not be.

Maybe this should be an Epic Idea, so you have a meta epic idea which will have wiki links to many smaller ideas, which will implemented in many releases.
