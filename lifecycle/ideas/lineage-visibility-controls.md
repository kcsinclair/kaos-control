# Lineage Visibility Controls

Lineage should become a first-class concept in the UI, surfaced more prominently across artifact tables and list views — showing the lineage chain each artifact belongs to as a visible, filterable column or grouping.

In graph views, lineage should be toggleable as a node/edge overlay, similar to how labels can be shown or hidden. Users should be able to opt in to seeing lineage nodes and relationships rendered in the graph, giving a structural view of how ideas flow through to requirements, plans, and tests.

This change elevates lineage from a metadata detail into a navigational and organisational tool, helping users understand the shape of their work at a glance.
